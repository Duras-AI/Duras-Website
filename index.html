About Duras AI

Duras AI is a technology startup based in Botswana, dedicated to solving the critical issue of human-elephant conflict (HEC) through innovative AI solutions.

Our name "Duras" comes from the Setswana word for "protect" or "guard," reflecting our mission to safeguard both communities and wildlife.

Every year, human-elephant conflicts result in destroyed crops, economic losses for farmers, and tragic outcomes for elephants. We're changing this narrative with technology that enables proactive prevention rather than reactive response.

Our Solution

Innovative technology for wildlife conservation

Autonomous Drone Patrols

Our drones conduct automated patrols at high-risk times (dawn, dusk, and night) when human-elephant conflicts are most likely to occur.

AI-Powered Detection

Using custom-trained computer vision models, our system accurately identifies elephants in real-time from drone video feeds.

Instant Alert System

When elephants are detected, instant SMS alerts are sent to community rangers and farmers, enabling timely, non-lethal intervention.
